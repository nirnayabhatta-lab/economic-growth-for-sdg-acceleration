# Nepal Policy Mapping Note

Nepal is treated as the anchor country for v1 because the platform is designed to show how growth, public finance, implementation, and transition pressures interact in a live policy setting.

The Nepal-specific mapping in v1 emphasizes:
- smooth transition from LDC graduation
- hydropower to productive-use conversion
- jobs and export competitiveness
- women's economic participation
- local capital execution and service delivery
- SDG financing and public investment quality
- climate resilience and territorial inclusion

The policy page is designed to show:
- lead institutions
- time horizon
- growth channels
- direct SDG links
- indirect SDG links
- implementation status
